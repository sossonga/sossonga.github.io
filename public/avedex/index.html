Avedex

The real-world bird version of a Pokedex.

Welcome to Hiking Blue Heron’s Avedex!

What is an Avedex?

Great question! It is the real-world version of a Pokedex, focused on birds in particular.

Each bird that is found on an adventure gets an entry in the Avedex. Not all entries have a picture of the bird when the bird is first added, those often get added later.
