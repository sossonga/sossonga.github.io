About

I started birding mid-2021 and it completely changed my view of the world. The Great Blue Heron is my spark bird.

My top two favorite hobbies are birding and hiking. It just so happens that both can be done at the same time. The goal of this blog is to share information about birds in a fun format and to talk about hikes in the New England (USA) area.
